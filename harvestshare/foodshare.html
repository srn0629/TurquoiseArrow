Welcome Harvest Community Gardeners

Share excess produce with your neighbors! Locate your garden with the green locate button or by zooming in. Select the produce you have to give from the panel. Tap your location on the map to add your produce. Click the "What to do..." icon for additional help. Delete entry when food is retrieved. Please only add, edit or delete items for your garden entries.
