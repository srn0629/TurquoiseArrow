ParksROCKS! - Hackathon

1 of 12 project submissions for the 3-day 'Metro Parks Tacoma Hackathon, Parks and People United through Technology', Parks ROCKS! took an innovate spin on engaging the Tacoma ROCKS! community with local parks. Using ArcGIS software, tools and online capabilities, we developed a map application for rockers to post hidden and found rocks! This project and its 6 contributors, myself included, from the local tech community earned 1st place at the hackathon event! Explore more of our project here!
